## üèÜ Competitive Advantages

### vs. Traditional Delivery Apps (Glovo, Uber Eats)
- **Content-first approach** vs. transaction-first
- **Cultural storytelling** vs. menu listings
- **Community features** vs. individual orders
- **Restaurant empowerment** vs. high commissions
- **Discovery focus** vs. search focus

### vs. Review Sites (TripAdvisor, Yelp)
- **Visual-first** vs. text-heavy
- **Story-driven** vs. rating-focused
- **Community engagement** vs. passive reviews
- **Kenyan-specific** vs. generic
- **Mobile-native** vs. web-first

### vs. Social Media (Instagram, Facebook)
- **Food-specific** vs. general social
- **Structured discovery** vs. feed-based
- **Action-oriented** vs. passive browsing
- **Restaurant tools** vs. personal profiles
- **Integrated booking** vs. external links

## üí∞ Revenue Model

### Primary Revenue Streams

1. **Premium Restaurant Listings** (30% of revenue)
   - Featured placement
   - Enhanced profiles
   - Analytics dashboard
   - Marketing tools

2. **Commission on Reservations** (25% of revenue)
   - Table bookings
   - Event bookings
   - Group orders
   - Corporate accounts

3. **Sponsored Content** (20% of revenue)
   - "Dish of the Day" promotions
   - Featured restaurants
   - Event sponsorships
   - Influencer partnerships

4. **Delivery Commission** (15% of revenue)
   - Optional delivery service
   - Lower commission than competitors
   - Value-added service, not core

5. **Premium User Features** (5% of revenue)
   - Ad-free experience
   - Early access to new restaurants
   - Exclusive food events
   - Advanced filters

6. **Gift Cards & Vouchers** (5% of revenue)
   - Restaurant gift cards
   - Platform vouchers
   - Corporate packages

### Monetization Philosophy
- **Restaurant-friendly** - Lower commissions, more value
- **User-first** - Free core features, premium optional
- **Sustainable** - Multiple revenue streams reduce risk
- **Growth-focused** - Revenue scales with community size
