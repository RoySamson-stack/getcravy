## üöß **Risks & Mitigation**

### **Risk 1: Restaurants Don't Sign Up**
**Mitigation:**
- Make it 100% free initially
- Do the setup work for them (you input menu, photos)
- Show value immediately (promote their events for free)
- Personal outreach, not cold emails

### **Risk 2: Users Don't Download**
**Mitigation:**
- Strong incentive (500 KES voucher)
- Campus activation where we demo the app in person
- Partner with influencers for credibility
- Solve real problem (finding events is currently hard)

### **Risk 3: Low Engagement/Retention**
**Mitigation:**
- Push notifications for events they'd like
- Weekly "This Weekend in Nairobi" roundup
- Gamification (points for trying new restaurants)
- Continuous feature improvements based on feedback

### **Risk 4: Competitors Copy Us**
**Mitigation:**
- Move fast (launch before they notice)
- Build relationships (restaurants are sticky)
- Focus on quality (better curation than competitors)
- Community building (users become advocates)

## üéØ **When to Add Video Later**

**Don't add video until you have:**
- ‚úÖ 5,000+ active users
- ‚úÖ 50+ restaurants posting events regularly
- ‚úÖ 10%+ booking conversion rate
- ‚úÖ $2,000+/month in revenue
- ‚úÖ $50,000+ funding for infrastructure

**Then add video as:**
- **GoEat Stories:** 24-hour restaurant/event highlights (like Instagram Stories)
- **Event Recaps:** User-generated content from events they attended
- **Dish Spotlights:** 15-second professional clips of signature dishes
- **NOT as:** Full TikTok clone (too expensive, too complex)

**Why wait:**
- Video hosting costs $500-2,000/month at scale
- Requires content moderation team
- Needs sophisticated algorithm
- Distracts from core value (discovery)
- Can add later once proven demand
